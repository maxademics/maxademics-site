Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Maxademics ("we", "our", "us") operates maxademics.com and the Canvas accessibility fixer at fix.maxademics.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

Files you upload. When you use the Canvas accessibility fixer, your file is sent to our backend (hosted on Hugging Face Spaces), processed in memory, and returned to you as a remediated file. We do not store, archive, or share uploaded files. Files are deleted from server memory immediately after the response is returned.

Newsletter sign-ups. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address via Tally.so. We use it only to send occasional updates about new tools and resources. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email.

Usage data. Like most websites, we log basic request information (IP address, browser type, referring page, pages visited) for security and analytics purposes.

2. Cookies and tracking

This site uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Google AdSense — displays personalized or non-personalized ads. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site or other sites.
  • Analytics — we may use Vercel Analytics or Google Analytics to understand which pages are popular and how visitors find us.
  • Functional cookies — used only when needed for site features.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info. You can also disable cookies in your browser settings, though some site features may not work properly.

3. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdSense — serves advertisements. See Google's Ads Policy.
  • Vercel — hosts the website. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Hugging Face — hosts the backend accessibility fixer. See Hugging Face's Privacy Policy.
  • Tally.so — hosts our newsletter form. See Tally's Privacy Policy.
  • Amazon Associates — affiliate links to books and products. See Amazon's Privacy Notice.

4. Affiliate disclosure

Some links on this site are affiliate links, which means we may earn a small commission (at no extra cost to you) if you make a purchase through them. We only recommend products we genuinely think are useful for teachers. As an Amazon Associate, we earn from qualifying purchases.

5. Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

6. Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at maxademics@gmail.com.

7. Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at maxademics@gmail.com.
